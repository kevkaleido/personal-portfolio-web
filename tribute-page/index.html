Christopher Ifekandu Okigbo

The poet who fought for freedom

Here's a timeline of Christopher Okigbo's life:

  • 1932 - Born in Ojoto, near Onitsha, Anambra State, Nigeria.
  • 1956 - Graduates from University College Ibadan with a degree in Classics.
  • 1962 - Publishes "Heavensgate", a profound collection of poems.
  • 1963 - Becomes West African Representative of Cambridge University Press in Ibadan.
  • 1964 - Celebrates the birth of his daughter, Obiageli, with "Dance of the Painted Maidens".
  • 1965 - Writes "Path of Thunder", foretelling political unrest.
  • 1967 - Joins Biafran military and dies in battle defending Nsukka.
